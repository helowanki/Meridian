Features

DirectX to Metal translation layer, native Apple Silicon support (M1, M2, M3+), controller support, and low-level graphics optimizations.

🧪

In Progress

Metal-based Ray Tracing (M3+), HDR rendering, Game Mode integration, Spatial Audio improvements, and performance overlays.
